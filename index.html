Researcher in the department of Computer Science at the University of Konstanz.

I am highly interested in computer science as well as mathematics and physics. Specifically, my research areas are Machine Learning for mathematics and computer vision.

Currently, I am working in the Visual Computing Group (Prof. Oliver Deussen) as well as in the Information Science Group (Prof. Bela Gipp).

In the past, I was working i.a. at TAU, DESY, PSI, and CERN.

Research

My main research interests are Neural Networks with special topologies, Mathematical Information Retrieval, and Computer Vision.

I am working on integrating smooth differentiable algorithms into neural networks and unsupervised solving of inverse problems. Additionally, I develop mathematical language processing techniques and novel blockchain technologies.
